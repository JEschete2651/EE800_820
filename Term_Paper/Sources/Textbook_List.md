# Textbook List from Lesson Plan Modules 1-5

This list consolidates the textbook-style resources referenced in Lesson Plan Modules 1 through 5. Items are deduplicated and annotated with the module(s) where they appear.

## Consolidated Textbook and Book-Length Resources

1. P. P. Chu, *FPGA Prototyping by VHDL Examples: Xilinx Artix-7 Edition*, 2nd ed., Wiley, 2017.
   Modules: 1, 2, 3
   Focus: FPGA prototyping, VHDL design, UART/FIFO implementation, hardware verification
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=FPGA%20Prototyping%20by%20VHDL%20Examples%20Xilinx%20Artix-7%20Edition%20P%20P%20Chu

2. R. E. Haskell and D. M. Hanna, *Digital Design Using Digilent FPGA Boards: VHDL / Vivado Edition*, LBE Books, 2019.
   Modules: 1, 2
   Focus: Digilent/Nexys A7 workflows, VHDL, Vivado, board-level examples
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Digital%20Design%20Using%20Digilent%20FPGA%20Boards%20VHDL%20Vivado%20Edition%20R%20E%20Haskell%20D%20M%20Hanna

3. C. Unsalan and B. Tar, *Digital System Design with FPGA: Implementation Using Verilog and VHDL*, McGraw-Hill, 2017.
   Modules: 1
   Focus: FPGA-based digital system design
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Digital%20System%20Design%20with%20FPGA%20Implementation%20Using%20Verilog%20and%20VHDL%20C%20Unsalan%20B%20Tar

4. B. Sklar, *Digital Communications: Fundamentals and Applications*, 2nd ed., Prentice Hall, 2001.
   Modules: 1
   Focus: digital communications fundamentals, modulation, link budgets
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Digital%20Communications%20Fundamentals%20and%20Applications%20B%20Sklar

5. J. W. Valvano, *Embedded Systems: Real-Time Interfacing to ARM Cortex-M Microcontrollers*, 6th ed., 2023.
   Modules: 1, 3
   Focus: STM32/ARM embedded interfacing, UART debugging, real-time microcontroller systems
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Embedded%20Systems%20Real-Time%20Interfacing%20to%20ARM%20Cortex-M%20Microcontrollers%20J%20W%20Valvano

6. P. J. Ashenden, *The Designer's Guide to VHDL*, 3rd ed., Morgan Kaufmann, 2008.
   Modules: 2
   Focus: VHDL language and design methodology
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=The%20Designer%27s%20Guide%20to%20VHDL%20P%20J%20Ashenden

7. W. McKinney, *Python for Data Analysis*, 3rd ed., O'Reilly, 2022.
   Modules: 3
   Focus: pandas, data cleaning, exploratory data analysis
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Python%20for%20Data%20Analysis%20W%20McKinney

8. A. Geron, *Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow*, 3rd ed., O'Reilly, 2022.
   Modules: 3, 4, 5
   Focus: ML workflows, preprocessing, model evaluation, end-to-end ML projects
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Hands-On%20Machine%20Learning%20with%20Scikit-Learn%20Keras%20and%20TensorFlow%20A%20Geron

9. C. M. Bishop and H. Bishop, *Deep Learning: Foundations and Concepts*, Springer, 2024.
   Modules: 4
   Focus: neural networks, backpropagation, regularization
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Deep%20Learning%20Foundations%20and%20Concepts%20C%20M%20Bishop%20H%20Bishop

10. T. Hastie, R. Tibshirani, and J. Friedman, *The Elements of Statistical Learning*, 2nd ed., Springer, 2009.
    Modules: 4
    Focus: statistical learning, random forests, support vector machines
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=The%20Elements%20of%20Statistical%20Learning%20Hastie%20Tibshirani%20Friedman

11. J. Quinonero-Candela et al., *Dataset Shift in Machine Learning*, MIT Press, 2009.
    Modules: 4
    Focus: covariate shift, prior probability shift, concept drift
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Dataset%20Shift%20in%20Machine%20Learning%20Quinonero-Candela

12. B. Beizer, *Software Testing Techniques*, 2nd ed., Van Nostrand Reinhold, 1990.
    Modules: 5
    Focus: stress testing, boundary analysis, combinatorial testing
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Software%20Testing%20Techniques%20Beizer

13. J. A. Whittaker, *Exploratory Software Testing*, Addison-Wesley, 2009.
    Modules: 5
    Focus: exploratory and system-level test design
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Exploratory%20Software%20Testing%20Whittaker

14. M. Alley, *The Craft of Scientific Presentations*, 2nd ed., Springer, 2013.
    Modules: 5
    Focus: technical presentation design
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=The%20Craft%20of%20Scientific%20Presentations%20M%20Alley

15. J. G. Paradis and M. L. Zimmerman, *The MIT Guide to Science and Engineering Communication*, 2nd ed., MIT Press, 2002.
    Modules: 5
    Focus: technical writing, engineering communication
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=The%20MIT%20Guide%20to%20Science%20and%20Engineering%20Communication%20Paradis%20Zimmerman

16. N. Weste and D. Harris, *CMOS VLSI Design: A Circuits and Systems Perspective*, 4th ed., Addison-Wesley, 2010.
    Modules: 5
    Focus: cross-layer digital system design trade-offs

    Focus: embedded and cyber-physical systems modeling across hardware, firmware, and software
   Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Introduction%20to%20Embedded%20Systems%20A%20Cyber-Physical%20Systems%20Approach%20Lee%20Seshia
